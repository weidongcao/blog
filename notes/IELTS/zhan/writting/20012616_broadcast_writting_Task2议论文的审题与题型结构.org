*** 主体段的写作
    全篇文章写3 - 4个观点足以

    Begining -- view of point
    1. Background
    2. Transition
    3. View of Point
    4. 40 words -3 Sentence

    Body -- supporting ideas: 3 - 4:
    1. Topic sentence
    2. Supporting ideas
    3. Transitional sentence
    4. 180 words 10sentences

    Ending --repetition of the opening
    1. 30 words -2/3 sentences
